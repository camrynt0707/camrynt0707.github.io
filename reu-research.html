REU Research: UX for Marginalized Online Daters

Project Overview

  • Timeline: Summer REU Program, University of Michigan–Flint
  • Role: Undergraduate Researcher
  • Team: 2 undergrads, grad students, professor

Problem Statement / Challenge

Online dating presents unique challenges for Deaf, Hard of Hearing, Visually Impaired, and Autistic adults...

Process & Methods

  • Project 1: Reflexive thematic analysis for D/HH/VI daters
  • Project 2: Survey design & thematic analysis for Autistic online daters
  • Project 3: Participatory design—survey for autistic adults about consent and technology solutions

Solutions & Deliverables

  • Thematic maps of barriers/issues
  • Feature insights for consent-awareness in tech
  • Example survey/user journey narratives

Lessons Learned

  • Flexible project management
  • Rigorous qualitative analysis & multidisciplinary teamwork
